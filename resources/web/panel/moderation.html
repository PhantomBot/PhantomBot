Enable/Disable Moderation Protection

Decide which moderation settings should be enforced and not enforced on all users below a moderator in chat. Users in the Regular and Subscriber groups may be made exempt from enabled settings. Disabling settings here completely disables the type of moderation.

Enable/Disable Moderation Protection for Regulars

Decide which moderation settings should be enforced and not enforced on Regulars in chat. If a specific moderation type is enabled but disabled here for Regulars, Regulars are exempt from that moderation type.

Enable/Disable Moderation Protection for Subscribers

Decide which moderation settings should be enforced and not enforced on Subscribers in chat. If a specific moderation type is enabled disabled here for Subscribers, Subscribers are exempt from that moderation type.
